Site Purpose

Sapporo Chamber of Commerce is based on Sapporo city Japan, and aim for a better local community by improving and empowering local welfare and businesses in Sapporo. We offer workshops and and educational event to members and contribute overral well-being.

What is the benefit of joining the chamber of commerce?

We frequently hold educational program to both big companies and small businesses. By joining us your can campany is able to improve businesses skill. Also, by engaging those activity, you can strength the relationship to companies around us, and we believe that strong business relationship between us contribute well-being this region.

What kind of events take place by the chamber of commerce?

We have a lot of activities to enhance business proper. We have workshops and seminars often. We also conduct PR campaings. To check the current event, please visit our event section page from this site.

How can I find a contact information of director of the chamber of commerce?

In the bottom of our website, we are providing contact information. You can also send us a message through message box in this website. Additionaly, you can also find important information or send a mesage from Instagram and Facebook.
